#### Ex 4
- regex to tokens
	- $\texttt{abba}\rightarrow$ keyword
	- $\texttt{aab}\rightarrow$ keyword
	- `[ab]+[01]*` -> ID {name}
	- `[+-]?[01]+` -> Numeral {value}
- Use Lexer generator to build NFA
- Or create NFA union of regexes with labeled final states
- NFA $\rightarrow$ DFA
